Our Services

Watch Repair

Our certified watchmakers provide expert repair services for all types of watches, from battery replacements to complete overhauls. We use only genuine parts and stand behind our work with a 1-year warranty on all repairs.

  • Battery replacement
  • Crystal replacement
  • Movement servicing
  • Water resistance testing
  • Band adjustment/replacement

Watch Restoration

Bring new life to your cherished timepieces with our professional restoration services. Whether it's a family heirloom or a vintage find, our specialists can restore your watch to its former glory while preserving its original character.

  • Case polishing/refinishing
  • Dial restoration
  • Hand replacement
  • Vintage movement servicing
  • Authentic part sourcing

Appraisal & Authentication

Our experts can provide certified appraisals for insurance purposes or resale valuation. We also offer authentication services to verify the authenticity of luxury timepieces, complete with detailed documentation.

  • Resale valuations
  • Luxury watch authentication
  • Certificates of authenticity
  • Market value assessment

Make your watch truly unique with our customization services. From engraving to bespoke modifications, we can help you create a one-of-a-kind timepiece that reflects your personal style.

  • Personal engraving
  • Custom dial design
  • Band customization
  • Special finishes
  • Bespoke watch creation
